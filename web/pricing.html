Frequently asked questions

Is Clauge really free?

The core features are free for personal, hobby, academic, and other noncommercial use — every mode listed under the Free plan above, with no paywalls. Clauge AI managed assistance, unlimited coworkers, and premium themes require the Pro tier. Any use by or within a for-profit organization — even just the core features — requires a separate license; contact us to discuss terms.

What's the difference between Pro and Enterprise?
  • Pro is per-user — premium add-ons for individuals on top of the free core.
  • Enterprise is per-organization — a commercial license for using Clauge at a for-profit company, plus bulk Pro entitlements, priority support, security review, and an optional private sync backend on your own Cloudflare account.

If you're a single dev, Pro is what you want. If you're deploying Clauge across a team, talk to us via the Enterprise form.
